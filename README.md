## Hello! 👋

I'm Bernardo Ribeiro, an undergraduate Chemical Engineering student at the Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ), based in Brazil.

I am building an interdisciplinary path between engineering, sustainability, data analysis and applied computing. My interests include environmental monitoring, life cycle assessment, circular economy, climate justice and the use of digital tools to support better decision-making.

I am currently developing my programming skills, especially in Python, while exploring how computation can be applied to research, environmental data, industrial processes and real-world problem solving.

I am curious, proactive and committed to learning. I enjoy connecting technical knowledge with practical challenges and I am always looking for opportunities to grow through projects, research, extension activities and collaboration.
